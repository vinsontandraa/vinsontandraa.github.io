CLOVES

Cloves is aromatic flower buds of a tree in the family Myrtaceae, Syzygium aromaticum... They are native to the Maluku Islands (or Moluccas) in Indonesia, and are commonly used as a spice. It's also very well known for its culinary purposes but it also has health benefits.

NUTMEG

Nutmeg is a popular spice made from the seeds of Myristica fragrans, a tropical evergreen tree native to Indonesia It can be found in wholeseed form but is most often sold as a ground spice.

CINNAMON

Cinnamon is used mainly as an aromatic condiment and flavouring additive in a wide variety of cuisines, sweet and savoury dishes, breakfast cereals, snackfoods, tea and traditional foods.

PALM SUGAR

Palm sugar is a sweetener derived from any variety of palm tree. Palm sugar is sometimes qualified by the type of palm, as in coconut palm sugar.... The predominant sources of palm sugar are the Palmyra, date, nipa, sugar and coconut palms.

ANDALIMAN
